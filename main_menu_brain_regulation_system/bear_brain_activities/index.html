Calm Cards

Use these simple strategies when the "Bear Brain" (survival mode) takes over to help return to safety.

Step-by-Step • 20 Seconds

Gentle Rock

"Gentle rhythm helps reconnect the nervous system."

1

Sit or stand calmly next to the child.

2

Slowly rock side to side in a steady rhythm.

3

Breathe deeply together while moving.

What to say

"I'm here with you. Let's take slow breaths together."

Sensory Check

Five Senses Grounding

"Grounding reconnects the brain to the present moment."

5 Seen
4 Felt
3 Heard
2 Smelled
1 Tasted

What to say

"Let's look around and notice what's here together."

Movement • 3 Repeats

Stretch and Reach

"Gentle movement wakes up the body's safety system."

  • check_circle Reach arms up high to the sky
  • check_circle Stretch slowly as if touching clouds
  • check_circle Lower arms gently back to sides

What to say

"Let's stretch our bodies slowly."
