3 Calm Cards

Techniques to help regulate big emotions and high energy.

Push the Wall

Pressure

Steps

  1. 1. Stand facing a wall
  2. 2. Place both hands flat
  3. 3. Push strongly (20s)
  4. 4. Release and take a deep breath

Why: Deep pressure releases excess activation.

Say: "I see your body has a lot of energy right now. Let's help it settle together."

Jump and Freeze

Movement

Steps

  1. 1. Jump as high as you can (10s)
  2. 2. Freeze like a stone statue
  3. 3. Take 3 slow breaths
  4. 4. Repeat 2 more times

Why: Movement releases built-up energy.

Say: "Let's jump the big energy out of your body."

Butterfly Hug

Calming

Steps

  1. 1. Cross your arms over your chest
  2. 2. Tap shoulders alternating Left/Right
  3. 3. Breathe slowly and deeply
  4. 4. Continue for 20-30 seconds

Why: Bilateral tapping calms the nervous system.

Say: "Let's give our body a calming butterfly hug."
